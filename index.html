About Us

Have you ever tried to measure something that seemed unmeasurable? At Automated Research Tools, LLC, we design experiments and develop custom tools to help you uncover answers and move forward with confidence. Whether you need data in extreme conditions, are gathering benchmarks for a project, or want to automate a repetitive research task, we’re here to help. Your work inspires us—and we’re proud to support breakthrough research, one solution at a time.

Standard Services

Customized Small-Scale Sensor Kits

We build custom, plug-and-play sensor kits that make it easy to collect and monitor data for research projects. Whether you're tracking renewable energy output, environmental conditions, or equipment performance, our kits deliver real-time insights with minimal setup. Designed for scientists, engineers, and research professionals, each kit is tailored to your needs—providing reliable, low-friction data collection so you can focus on what matters.

Robotic Attachments

Boost your research capabilities with custom robotic attachments designed to enhance and automate complex experimental workflows. From motorized mounts to precision-actuated components, our designs integrate with your existing lab or field equipment—eliminating the need for full robotic systems. As design consultants, we provide plug-and-play tools to make your experiments faster and more consistent, without overhauling your setup. All solutions are intended for research and prototyping use.

Our Mission

At Automated Research Tools, LLC, our mission is to empower researchers and innovators with accessible, automated tools for data collection and process improvement. We aim to simplify complex measurement tasks, reduce manual effort, and accelerate discovery. By developing reliable, user-friendly solutions, we help bridge the gap between cutting-edge technology and practical research needs.

Meet the Founder

Our founder, Ian Seremet, is a Mechanical Engineering senior at the University of Minnesota with a passion for design and robotics. Automated Research Tools, LLC was born at the intersection of these interests—combining creative problem-solving with practical automation to help research teams develop smarter, more efficient tools for their work.
